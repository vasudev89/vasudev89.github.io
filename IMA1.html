IMA1 : Job Aid for using Augmented reality for metaphor comprehension

   

Performance problem analysis: As part of the instructions for the assignment IMA1, I created a job aid for enabling language teachers to use an Augmented reality (AR) app for understanding abstract literary concepts, such as metaphors.

   

The need for using the AR app arises from students finding it challenging to grasp difficult concepts related to figurative language. An AR app, LiterAR, would help teachers guide students to use the app and benefit from the AR images to appreciate the metaphor in the story. The teachers are technologically-savvy, but have not used the app before. The job aid helps them facilitate the learning process by using the app effectively. You can read the details of the performance problem analysis here.

   

Description of the job aid: The job aid has been designed using Adobe Photoshop and highlights the sequential process of using the AR app. The design is simple and includes step-wise instructions to use the app. I designed images using the tools of Adobe Photoshop to depict the instructional intent of each step. I also used real-life images to highlight the way the app is used. You can download the job aid here.

   

Educational value of the job aid: The instructional intent of the job aid is to simplify the use of a new technological intervention to help students learn with ease. AR is a bit overwhelming to use for beginners because of its novelty and the difficulty of understanding its utility.

   

By using the job aid, teachers can help students understand complex concepts using multi-sensory learning and contextual learning. AR is a new technology and teachers can use the app to explain complex literary concepts and figurative language to help students truly "figure it out!"
